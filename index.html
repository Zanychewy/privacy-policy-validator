Privacy Policy for Content Origin Validator

This browser extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

  • We do not track your browsing history.
  • We do not collect any personal identifiers such as your name, email, or IP address.
  • All analysis and fingerprinting of content is performed locally within your browser.
  • Only anonymized hashes of visible content may be sent to a public registry API for validation, without any identifying metadata.
